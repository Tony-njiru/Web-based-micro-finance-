Q.What is matrixfund?
A. matrixfund is a peer-to-peer donation and mutual aid fund scheme for members to help other members in an efficient way. By using this scheme, members gives and receives donations from each other.

Q.What is the aim of matrixfund?
A. matrixfund was created to promote the greatest good, with a particular emphasis on helping man. Its connect people through donation to provide financial assistance to each other and everybody is helped.

Q.Do I have to refer someone to earn?
A. No referral link so u don't have to invite anyone before getting paid

Q.What is the difference between the Plans?
